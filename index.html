WHAT IS LINK IN BLINK ?

What is LinkinBlink?

LinkinBlink is an innovative platform dedicated to enhancing user security and convenience by digitizing and encrypting identity verification processes.


LinkinBlink is an app designed to streamline and secure various processes for users by integrating with India's Aadhar API. It offers features that eliminate the need for physical documentation, providing seamless and secure experiences.

Why Choose LinkinBlink ?

Security:

Protects sensitive data with encryption and live verification.

Convenience:

Eliminates the hassle of carrying and sharing physical Aadhar cards.

Trust:

Provides a safe and reliable method for identity sharing and verification.

How LinkinBlink Works ?

Digital Aadhar Sharing:

Users no longer need to share their physical Aadhar card. LinkinBlink allows them to securely share their e-Aadhar in an encrypted format, ensuring safety and privacy.

Encryption for Enhanced Security:

All e-Aadhar data shared with vendors through the app is encrypted, safeguarding users from potential misuse.

Hotel Booking Verification:

Users provide their booking ID for hotel stays.LinkinBlink verifies their booking by cross-referencing it with their Aadhar card number.

Live Face Detection:

The app employs live face detection technology to authenticate users in real-time, ensuring the individual sharing the Aadhar card is its rightful owner.

Secure Sharing:

After successful verification, users receive an encrypted version of their Aadhar card. This can then be securely shared with any vendor or service provider as required.

With LinkinBlink, your data stays protected while you enjoy seamless, secure interactions with hotels, events, and vendors.

The Solution Behind LinkinBlink

Aadhar API Integration

The app securely connects with the Aadhar system to verify users' identities. This ensures authenticity and compliance with official standards.

End-to-End Encryption

All user data, including e-Aadhar cards, is encrypted both during storage and transmission, making it virtually impossible for unauthorized parties to access or misuse your information.

Live Face Detection Technology

Advanced biometric verification ensures that only the rightful owner of the Aadhar card can access and share it.

Secure Sharing Protocols

When users share their e-Aadhar with vendors or services, it’s done using encrypted pathways, preventing tampering or interception.

Is My Data Safe?

Encryption

Your e-Aadhar and other sensitive details are encrypted using industry-leading algorithms. Even if intercepted, the data is unreadable without the decryption key.

Access Control

Only authorized parties (e.g., vendors or service providers) with your explicit consent can access the shared data.

No Physical Risk

By eliminating the need for physical Aadhar cards, the app protects you from the risks of loss, theft, or misuse of physical copies.

Regulatory Compliance

LinkinBlink adheres to all necessary data protection regulations, ensuring that your privacy is prioritized and safeguarded.

Do I have to Register for every travel ?

Registration/ enrolment to the link in blink App is a one-time process. You don’t have to register every time you travel. For each new travel instance, simply add the new travel document and share your credentials prior to your travel.

Why is only Aadhar accepted as the Govt ID?

At this time, the link in blink app accepts only AADHAAR as it has been integrated, however, we plan to integrate additional government issued identity documents in the future.
